Oslo Afro Arts festival

Welcome to the official website of Oslo Afro Arts. We are delighted to announce the return of our annual main event, Oslo Afro Arts Festival in 2021. The 7th edition of the Festival will be held from 26-29 August 2021 in Oslo, Norway, We aim to bring you an exciting festival enriched with all the sights, sounds and vibes that can only be experienced at our festival. Up and coming young artists interested in performing are invited to contact us. More information will be posted in the coming days and weeks. In addition to this website, please check out our Instagram and Facebook for the latest updates.
